5. NFC and RFID

5.1. RFID: Radio-Frequency Identification

two components

  • Tags: passive or active
  • Readers: usually connected to computer system

5.2. NFC: Near Field Communication

subset of RFID, designed for close range usually within 4 cm or less

Diff:

  • RFID could generally work on meters range
  • NFC simpler and more consumer-oriented
  • RFID usually used for tracking item, while NFC used for contactless payments
